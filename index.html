Sales Performance Platform — Direct Sales Organisation (Anonymised)

A direct sales company managing 500+ field representatives across the UK had no real-time visibility of performance. Commission calculations took several days each month and contained errors that were causing team disputes. Leadership made strategic decisions without reliable data.

Case study 02
UK Cost of Living Dashboard — Python ETL + Power BI

No accessible tool existed for professionals to compare real disposable income across UK cities — after accounting for rent, transport, and tax. The data existed across multiple ONS sources but had never been combined into a usable format.

We built a Python ETL pipeline ingesting live ONS salary and rent data across 10 cities, feeding a 3-page interactive Power BI dashboard with a real-time salary scenario planner — allowing anyone to input their salary and instantly see disposable income by city.

Case study 03
UK Data Jobs Market Analysis — Live API Pipeline + Power BI

No clear, data-driven picture existed of what the UK data job market actually looks like — salary ranges by role, geographic distribution of demand, and which companies are hiring most actively.

We built a Python ETL pipeline pulling live job listings from the Adzuna API, analysing 870 job listings across Data Analyst, BI Analyst, Data Engineer, and Data Scientist roles. A 3-page Power BI dashboard covers salary analysis, geographic mapping, and hiring trends — all from live, automated data.
